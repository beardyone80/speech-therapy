 Do you recognise any of these signs in your child?

  • Not Responding to His or Her Name
  • Stuttering
  • Failing to Follow Directions
  • Refusing to Speak
  • Indistinct Speaking

 We can help you with

  • Disfluency - Stuttering is the most common type of disfluency.
  • Articulation disorder - The child is not able to produce speech sounds clearly, such as saying "coo" instead of "school
  • Phonological disorder - The child does not use some or all of the speech sounds to form words as expected for their age
  • Voice disorders - Hoarseness or raspiness to the voice, pitch changes,
